Chilli Bird started in a kitchen in Fife. A Thai-born founder. A desire for the fragrant, aromatic cooking of home — and zero interest in the bland, heat-only chilli oils on UK shelves.

So she made her own. Lemongrass. Lime leaves. Toasted garlic. Bird's eye chillies. Handmade in small batches, clean ingredients, the real deal. Her friends wanted jars. Then their friends did.

Today it's still the same recipe, the same hands, the same obsession. Just more jars.
