03 / เรื่องราวของเรา — Our Story
Born In
A Home
Kitchen.
By a founder who just wanted it to taste like home.

Chilli Bird started in a kitchen in Fife. A desire for the fragrant, aromatic cooking of home — and zero interest in the bland, heat-only chilli oils on UK shelves.

So she made her own. Lemongrass. Lime leaves. Toasted garlic. Bird's eye chillies. Handmade in small batches, clean ingredients, the real deal. Her friends wanted jars. Then their friends did.

Today it's still the same recipe, the same hands, the same obsession. Just more jars.

04 / The Maker — เจ้าของ
The Woman
Behind
The Jar.
A Fife kitchen, a love of Thai food, and an obsession with getting it right.

She grew up eating food that tasted like it had a story. Moving to Scotland meant leaving those flavours behind — until she decided to recreate them herself.

Every batch is still made in the same kitchen. The same recipe. The same hands. The only thing that's changed is the number of people at the table.
