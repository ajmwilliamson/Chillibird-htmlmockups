Our Story
From a home kitchen
in Fife to every table
in Scotland

Chilli Bird was born from a simple hunger — not for heat, but for fragrance. Our founder grew up in Thailand surrounded by the scent of lemongrass sizzling in hot oil, lime leaves releasing their oils, garlic toasting golden.

"In Thailand, the aromatics come first. The heat is just the beginning."

When she moved to Scotland, she couldn't find a chilli oil that smelled like home. So she made one, in a kitchen in Fife, for friends and family. Then everyone started asking for jars.

Today, every jar is still made by hand in small batches — cleaner ingredients, no shortcuts, the same aromatics that made that first batch unforgettable.
