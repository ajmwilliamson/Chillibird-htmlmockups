II / Origin_Record.log — Reading

Born from a
Home Kitchen.
Built on Fragrance.

Chilli Bird began in a home kitchen in Fife — born from the desire to capture Thailand's aromatic, flavour-forward cooking and share it with the world.

Our founder grew up with the scent of lemongrass, lime leaves and toasted garlic filling the kitchen. Moving to Scotland, she found something missing — all heat, no soul. So she made her own.

Every jar is still handmade in small batches, using cleaner ingredients and the Thai aromatics that make all the difference. No shortcuts. No compromise.
